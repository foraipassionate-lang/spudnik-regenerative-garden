### 1. Adapt Protocols for Your Climate

Our protocols are designed for East Flanders (Zone 8b). If you adapt them for your climate:
- Document what you changed and why
- Share your local material sources
- Note timing adjustments for your frost dates
- Submit as a pull request or open an issue with your findings

### 2. Share Experimental Results

Tried one of our techniques? We want to know:
- What worked
- What didn't work
- What you'd do differently
- Photos if possible

Open an issue titled "Results: [Technique Name] in [Your Location]"

### 3. Improve Documentation

Found something unclear? Help us fix it:
- Typo corrections
- Clarifying confusing sections
- Adding diagrams or photos
- Translating into other languages

### 4. Add New Guild Designs

Have a polyculture guild that works well for you?
- Document the plants and their functions
- Explain the planting arrangement
- Share your climate context
- Include success/failure notes

### 5. Expand the Reference Library

Know of excellent permaculture/agroforestry resources?
- Ensure they're freely shareable (public domain, CC license, or author permission)
- Provide proper attribution
- Explain why it's valuable
