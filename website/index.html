🌍 Core Principles 🌍

💚

Culture > Market Cap

Community and relationships over transactions. We're cultivating living ecosystems, not extracting value.

🌱

Demonstrate > Sell

Show results before making claims. Potatoes don't scream underground - they quietly become essential.

♻

Waste = Resource

Every output becomes an input. Brewery grains become LAB. Sour kombucha becomes calcium extraction.

🧬

Feed Biology

Soil food web over synthetic inputs. We feed the microbes that feed the plants that feed us.
