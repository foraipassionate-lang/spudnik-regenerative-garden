## Companion Planting Strategy

### North Edge (Shade-Tolerant)
- **Horseradish:** 2 plants at NW and NE corners (pest deterrent fortress)

- **Nasturtiums:** 5-6 plants (aphid trap crop, edible flowers)
- **Bush beans:** 3-4 plants between nasturtiums (nitrogen fixation)
